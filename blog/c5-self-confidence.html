Self Confidence

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

From what I've understood mindfulness is self awareness at a broader scale. Self awareness teaches you to be conscious of your emotions and see yourself from a third person perspective. Mind fullness teaches you that as well as controlling you ego and increasing your self confidence through the methods of self awareness.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

I think that was my favorite part of the chapter. The principle behind it is that we shouldn't let our emotions define who we are and should only see them as a physical/mental reaction of our mind and body.
